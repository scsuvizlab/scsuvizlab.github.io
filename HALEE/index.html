AI Evolution Timeline: 1940s - 2045

1950: The Turing Test

Alan Turing proposes a test to determine if a machine can exhibit intelligent behavior.

Read more...

The Turing Test, proposed by Alan Turing in his paper "Computing Machinery and Intelligence," was designed to test a machine's ability to exhibit intelligent behavior equivalent to, or indistinguishable from, that of a human.

1956: The Dartmouth Conference

The field of AI is officially founded at a workshop at Dartmouth College.

Read more...

The Dartmouth Conference, officially the Dartmouth Summer Research Project on Artificial Intelligence, was hosted by John McCarthy and Marvin Minsky. This workshop is widely considered the founding event of artificial intelligence as a field.

1997: IBM's Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov

A computer defeats the world chess champion for the first time.

Read more...

IBM's Deep Blue became the first computer chess-playing system to win both a chess game and a chess match against a reigning world champion under regular time controls.

2011: IBM Watson Wins Jeopardy!

Watson defeats two of Jeopardy!'s greatest champions.

Read more...

IBM's question answering system Watson competed on Jeopardy! against former winners Brad Rutter and Ken Jennings. Watson won the first place prize of $1 million.

2012: ImageNet Breakthrough

Deep learning model achieves breakthrough in image recognition.

Read more...

AlexNet, a deep convolutional neural network, achieved a top-5 error of 15.3% in the ImageNet Large Scale Visual Recognition Challenge, significantly outperforming the runner-up (26.2% error).

2022: ChatGPT Released

OpenAI releases ChatGPT, bringing conversational AI to the mainstream.

Read more...

ChatGPT, based on the GPT-3.5 architecture, was released to the public in November 2022 and gained over a million users within 5 days, demonstrating unprecedented public interest in conversational AI.

2028: National AI Infrastructure Act

Legislation establishes framework for AI integration in critical infrastructure.

Read more...

Following multiple system failures and cyberattacks, the National AI Infrastructure Act established mandatory safety standards, testing protocols, and resilience requirements for AI systems managing critical infrastructure.

2035: Universal Basic Income Implementation

National UBI program launched in response to AI-driven job displacement.

Read more...

After widespread automation across service, transportation, and knowledge work sectors, the government implemented a comprehensive UBI program to address structural unemployment.
